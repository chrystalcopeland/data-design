Conceptual Model

As a power user, I want to search for pet friendly hotels, restaurants, and destinations for my trip.

  • Search for pet friendly destinations by location
  • Follow the search for destinations by pet friendly hotels
  • based on where I am staying, I would like to also find pet friendly restaurants
  • I would also like to save favorite locations to my profile to plan my trip
